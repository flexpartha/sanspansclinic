From Dr. Basu's Desk

Sanghamitra Basu, M.D

Sanghamitra Basu, M.D completed her medical school at the University of Arizona.She received her anesthesiology training at Rush Medical Center in Chicago, lllinois in 2004 and completed her fellowship in pain management at Rush University in Chicago, lllinois in 2005.

Dr. Basu has had extensive training in the management of spinal pain, headaches, neuropathic pain and CRPS pain. She also has extensive experience in spinal cord stimulation and intrathecal infusion systems.


Who Benefits From Our Services

  • Patients suffering from pain from any cause. People who have an injury that keeps hurting, unexplained pain, or headache, weakness or neurologic problems, or pain from cancer can be treated successfully.
  • Patients requiring increasing doses of pain medication, associated with depression, anxiety, or sleep disturbance.
  • Physicians who are treating patients not responding to appropriate therapy.
  • Employers experiencing lost days from their employees.
  • Attorneys trying to help their clients cope with disabilities.
